How it works

1 Point are awarded for each new footprint entered. The footprint will be veried to make sure it is correct and different from the other footprints already entered for this Gift. Once a footprint is verified another point is awarded.

Periodically points are converted into payment and marked as redeemed. The current conversion is 1c per point.
